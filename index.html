Claire Graham Portfolio

Claire is a freelance dancer based in Düsseldorf. She has been dancing professionally for three years, and choreographed various short pieces in that time. She received her main training at the Martha Graham school in New York, where she gined an in-depth knowledge of the modern dance technique, and knowledge of the spine as a whole. From there she went on to the Area Jeune Ballet, where she worked with various choreographers, and went on to work with Julio Arozarena as a soloist in his production of "Polymère" Today, she is working as a member of the the artist collective "Nowaday Dance Company".
